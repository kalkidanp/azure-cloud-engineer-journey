



🚀 Day 16: Security Upgrade — Azure Bastion!


Even on busy days, consistency wins. Today, I dove into **Azure Bastion** 💻 — the secure way to access VMs without exposing them to the internet.  


💡 Fun Analogy:
- Without Bastion: Your house door is wide open 🚪  
- With Bastion: You enter through a **guarded gate** 🔐 (secure, monitored, safe)  


What I explored:
- Why Bastion needs a dedicated subnet (`AzureBastionSubnet`)  
- How public IP allocation is secure while VM remains private  
- How it compares to traditional RDP/SSH (spoiler: much safer!)  
- Bicep practice in VS Code — hands-on coding without deployment 🚀


🔥 Question for you:  
If your VM had **no public IP and no Bastion**, how would you access it safely?  


Even when the day was long, I showed up and wrote the Bicep code, visualized traffic flows, and understood security concepts deeply. 💙
